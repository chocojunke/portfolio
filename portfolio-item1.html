PILLS DISPENSER

MICROCONTROLLER

Arduino and 3D Printing

Development of a pills dispenser to help elder people with missing pills take.

The components used in this project include Arduino, Arduino components (sensors, engines, leds, buttons and lcd), 3D development and 3D printing and database.

The quantity of pills and the times of intake were saved in an SQL Database.

At the dispense times, an alarm would sound to warn the person.

If the person didn't click the button, it would record that was a missing intake.

The physical machine was 3d designed by the group and printed at home.
